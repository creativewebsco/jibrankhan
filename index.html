About

Jibran Khan is a Lawyer holding an LLB from the University of London (Birkbeck). His legal practice primarily focuses on Criminal Law and Contract Law, while also extending to Corporate Matters, Civil Litigation, Immigration Law, International Law, Wills, and Probate. In addition to his legal practice, he is also a Human Rights Activist, committed to justice and social responsibility. He approaches every case with clarity, precision, and strong client-first advocacy, ensuring professional excellence and ethical representation.
